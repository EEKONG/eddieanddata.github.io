Python and Seaborn:
Weekly Sales Seasonality Patterns

This python visualization highlights the seasonal trends in walmart weekly sales across the years 2011, 2012, and 2013. The y-axis represents the log-transformed weekly sales (Weekly_Sales_Log10) to normalize scale and reduce skewness, while the x-axis represents the months of the year. Sales show clear seasonality, with sharp rises early in the year and strong holiday-driven spikes in November–December. Year-end growth was strongest in 2010–2011, while 2012 showed weaker momentum.

Key Takeaway:

  • The Walmart planning and inventory strategies team should prioritize early-year and holiday seasons, as they consistently drive the highest performance across years.
